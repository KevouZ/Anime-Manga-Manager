Welcome to your personal Anime & Manga Manager

You can list all your mangas or animes, filter them by genre, mark them as pending or completed, and much more...

For now, the available functionalities are:

  • Add anime or manga.
  • Mark as pending or completed.
  • Select genre of the anime or manga.
  • Delete anime or manga.

This project is still in development. If you want to contact me for support or suggestions, you can do so through: GitHub, Discord, Instagram, or Email.
